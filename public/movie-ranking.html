The Best Movies According to Jon

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dumb and Dumber

Perfectly stupid slap-stick comedy.

Terminatior 2: Judgment Day

Shows us how the world could be if we are not to careful with A.I.

A beautiful romantic comedy that teaches you that you shouldn't just be focused on work.

Amazing cast, amazing music, amazing car chase sceens.

Matrix

Cool sci-fi movie.

Die Hard
